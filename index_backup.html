BlockyCraft

BlockyCraft is a fan-made game of Blockman GO, created by ZNFDev with Comical and some additional help of Rosario. It serves as an older iteration of Blockman GO. Players can access a variety of games within BlockyCraft and have the ability to add and play with friends

Features

Multiplayer

Play online with others in BlockyCraft, teaming up with friends or challenging players globally in various game modes.

New Skins

BlockyCraft features exclusive "Galaxy Wings" skins, a unique cosmetic flair not found in Blockman GO.

Add Friends

Easily connect with and add friends you meet in BlockyCraft to organize games and enhance your cooperative experience.

Playable Games

Explore a wide array of diverse mini-games and experiences available to play in BlockyCraft.

Change Skins

Customize your look in BlockyCraft by easily changing your skin from a wide wardrobe of options.
